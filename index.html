RNMT-RAM S36p Supplementary Materials

Yijia Qiang, Lydia Hepburn, Victoria H. Cowling and Andrei Pisliakov

Supplementary movies and plots for molecular dynamics simulations of phosphorylation-dependent changes in the RNMT-RAM complex. The materials focus on RAM Ser36 phosphorylation and its interaction with RNMT interface residues H439 and K442.
